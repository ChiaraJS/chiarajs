15 Game

The Game of Fifteen is a sliding puzzle that consists on 4x4 board for a total of 16 slots. Fifteen slots are randomly numbered from 1 to 15 while one of them is empty. The object of the puzzle is to place the tiles in order by making sliding moves that use the empty space.
